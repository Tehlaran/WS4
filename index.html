About Me

Esther

Hi, I'm Esther, the baker behind this sweet little corner of the internet! I’ve been baking since I was 15 years old, starting with cakes for family and friends' birthdays and special celebrations. It all began in my hometown in Brazil, where I fell in love with the magic of mixing ingredients and creating something that could bring people together. What started as a hobby soon became a tradition—whether it was for a birthday, a holiday, or just because, I was always the one in the kitchen baking up something delicious for those I loved. Over the years, I perfected my craft, experimenting with flavors, textures, and designs, always trying to make each cake better than the last. Five years ago, I moved to Toronto, and that's when things really began to change. My friends here were constantly asking me to bake for them, and it didn't take long for me to realize how much I missed sharing my passion with others. So, with a little push from the people who mean the most to me, I decided to take the plunge and turn my love for baking into something more—my very own cake shop! Now, I'm here, combining my Brazilian roots with the vibrant energy of Toronto, and I'm so excited to share my cakes with you! Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or just treating yourself to something sweet, I’m here to create a cake that’ll make your day a little brighter. Thanks for stopping by—I hope to bake something special for you soon!
